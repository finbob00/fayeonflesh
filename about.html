About

Artistic Focus
I am a gothic‑focused artist specialising in horror‑inspired illustration, drawing influence from classic dark cinema such as Dracula, Nosferatu, and early expressionist film.
Style & Approach
My work blends clean linework, detailed composition, and a strong sense of atmosphere, whether created digitally or on paper.
Background
Art has been a central part of my life for years, and studying it in college strengthened both my technical ability and my commitment to developing as a professional creative.
Professional Values
I approach every piece with discipline, precision, and a focus on hygiene and safety — values I intend to carry into tattooing.
Tattoo Apprenticeship
I am working toward a tattoo apprenticeship to bring my style to skin. I’m drawn to tattooing not only for the art form itself, but for the craft, the culture, and the responsibility of creating permanent work for clients.
Customer Experience
My background in customer service has taught me how to communicate clearly, work respectfully with people, and maintain a calm, professional presence.
