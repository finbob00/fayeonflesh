About

Who I Am
I’m Faye Thomas — an artist specialising in gothic, horror‑inspired illustration, blackwork concepts, and tattoo‑ready designs. My work blends anatomical detail, dark stylisation, and expressive character design.
Artistic Background
I’ve spent years developing a style rooted in contrast, texture, and narrative. My influences range from classic horror cinema to anatomical illustration and gothic visual culture. I work both digitally and traditionally, always aiming for clarity, intention, and atmosphere.
What Drives My Work
I’m drawn to art that feels personal, symbolic, and emotionally charged. Whether creating portraits, creatures, or anatomical pieces, I focus on storytelling through shape, shadow, and expression.
Future Goals
My long‑term goal is to bring my style into tattooing — combining illustration with craft, discipline, and connection. I’m actively seeking apprenticeship opportunities and studio experience to grow professionally.
